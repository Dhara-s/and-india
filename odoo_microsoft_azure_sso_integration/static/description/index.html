USER GUIDE WITH SCREENSHOTS

Go to the Azure Portal. In the Azure Services click on the Azure Directory.

Go to 'App registrations'.

Click on the 'New Registration'.

Give an appropriate name and select 'Accounts in any Organizational directory (Multitenant)' option. Click on the Register button.

Click on the newly created Application.

Click on the Overview Menu. Copy the Application(Client) Id.

Go to the 'API Permissions'.

Select the permissions and click on the 'Update Permissions' button.

Go to the 'Certificates and Secrets' menu. Click on the 'New Client Secret' option.

Generate the Client Secret key.

Copy the generated 'Value'.

Go to the 'Overview' menu.

Click on the 'Redirected URIs' option.



Click on the 'Add a Platform' option.



Select 'Web Applications'.



Add the Redirected URI and click on the 'Configure' button.






Go to Odoo -> Settings and click on the 'Activate the developer mode' option.



Click on the 'OAuth Providers' option.



Click on the 'Microsoft OAuth2' option



Add the previously copied Microsoft Client ID and the Secret Key(Value).






Click on the 'Log in with Microsoft' option.



Enter the credentials.






On successful authentication you will be redirected to Odoo.
